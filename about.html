ABOUT ME

"Video Games, or your money back!"

My first exposure to video games came around 2009 when I recieved a hand-me-down PlayStation 2 but I didn't find the spark to actually create games until my GCSEs, where I found the combined magic of PC Gaming and Video Game Modding thanks to Half-Life 2 and its modding-friendly Source Engine.

Since that discovery, I began pursuing education in both Art and Technology, eventually landing in Ulster University and its Games Design course where I was given an even broader understanding of game development and design, also being able to go hands-on with a now industry standard tool in the Unreal Engine and its visual scripting system.

It's in this course that I found out about the process of "Rapid Prototyping", where I'm able to quickly iterate on and implement functions, mechanics and the like, it's grown to be my favorite method of game design due to its rapid throughput especially when it comes to testing.

With the hands-on experience with the Unreal Engine, my desire to create and experiment has only continued to grow, as I learn more about what I can do using the Unreal Engine, the more that I think about how I can repurpose that education for my own personal projects, projects not just specific to the Unreal Engine but also made on other engines.
