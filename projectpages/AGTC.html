GAMEPLAY VIDEO

BACKGROUND

"Annual Garbage Throwing Competition" is a Virtual Reality game developed within a team as an experiment in Games Design.

It was picked out of a list of potential projects that included other VR games as well as Mobile and Augmented Reality projects.

Developed within a 4-5 week timeframe, it was the first ever Virtual Reality game/project that I have ever worked on and my first real exposure to most of the fundamentals, subtleties & idiosyncrasies of VR games and their development.

ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

Within the team, I was the Sole Programmer of the project and was the one that had the most hands on time with the VR Headset.
