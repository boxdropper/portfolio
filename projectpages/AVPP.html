BACKGROUND

"A Very Postal Protest" is a First Person Shooter developed solo for "POSTAL Jam 2" by POSTAL Series developer Running With Scissors.

It was created during a period of downtime between University semesters and being a fan of the controversial series, I decided to contribute to the Game Jam.

This entry was developed within the space of a month and was among the first Unreal Engine-based project that I've released publically.

ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

This was a solo project, with the models, animations, scripts and level all done by me, with models and sound effects sourced from both the POSTAL games and free third-party resources.

CONTRIBUTIONS

• Creating Weapon and Arm Viewmodels for the Player.

• Modelled, Textured, Animated and Scripted a "Secret" Boss Encounter, with funtioning AI.

• Designed a Level using both Unreal Engine's Landscape and Cube-Grid Tools.

• Recorded a voiced intro cutscene for the main/playable Level.

• Rapid prototyping of the "Protest" mechanic and the ability to change sign design.
