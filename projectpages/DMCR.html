BACKGROUND

"Deathmatch Classic: Refragged" is a third-party, Source Engine powered, remake of the lesser-known Valve Multiplayer Game: "Deathmatch Classic", a spiritual successor to Quake's Multiplayer component.

I was granted access to the Closed Alpha to test back in July of 2024 and have remained since.
