BACKGROUND

"Jabroni Brawl: Episode 3" is a multiplayer shooter based on a licenced branch of the Source Engine featuring a wide assortment of maps, gamemodes, playermodels and weapons.

I was granted access to the game as a Closed Beta Tester in July 2020 and have remained part of the project ever since.

Testing is usually conducted on a bi-weekly basis using a separate "Beta Playtest" build via Steam, with feedback collated later on with screenshots and videos.

ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

Within the team, I have the role of bug-hunting, finding and recording bugs to submit to the devs for review, patching and further testing.
