BACKGROUND

"triplepuzzle." was a single Level Design entry for the ongoing Source Engine anthology project "Map Labs" and its "Three Rooms" challenge, planned, started and finished in about 4 days.

It is the second time that I have made a submission to Map Labs, the first being for their "DeceptionVile" Test Tube with RunThinkShootLive titled "videogame".

"triplepuzzle" was created for my love of the Source Engine and desire to do more with it other than small, private experiments.

ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

Since "triplepuzzle." was a solo project, I had the roles and responsibilities of both Planning and Implementing the Level Design

KEY SOFTWARE

Source SDK

Adobe Photoshop

CONTRIBUTIONS

• Rapid protyping of each of the three rooms, working on each separately/one at a time.

• Created custom textures using Photoshop and converted them to Source's proprietary Texture & Material Formats (VTF/VMT).

• Made usage of the Source Engine's Input/Output system to script and implement multiple endings and the actual functionality of the Puzzles.

• Ported assets from other Source Engine title, Counter-Strike: Source.
