BACKGROUND

The "Xenus 2 Hud Redesign" project was an attempt to redesign various pieces of the User Interface from the obscure 2008 Ukrainian First-Person RPG, "Xenus 2: White Gold", otherwise known as "White Gold: War In Paradise".

The goal was to produce a HUD that both communicates more of the mechanics of the game that were originally not easily displayed and to make it usuable with a Gamepad/Controller.

It begun with re-designing the HUD in the interface design software "Figma", then exported to Adobe Illustrator to later be exported to the Unreal Engine to be implemented and made functional.

CONTRIBUTIONS

• Conducted research on both the original UI and other video game UI to identify points of potential improvement.

• Brainstorming 3 Player "Personas" to set guidelines for redesigning.

• Creation of both Low and High Fidelity Wireframes of Interfaces such as the In-Game HUD and Inventory.

• Used Figma's Prototyping capabilities to make the redesign compatible with a Controller.

• Created a new logo for the game using Adobe Illustrator.
